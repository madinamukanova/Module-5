Cafe Central is a city cafe, a new center of ideas and creation in Almaty, where

the aroma of great coffee and the taste of a happy life are in perfect balance.

This is a place where the latest news and rumors are discussed, interesting

conversations are held, no less interesting meetings are held, and "delicious"

events are created.

The idea of the cafe came from the legendary cafe in Vienna, where all the

geniuses of Europe played chess for 2 centuries.
